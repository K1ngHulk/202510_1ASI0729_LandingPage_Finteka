About Us

Mission

Through its first product, the PlayMatch web app, the company offers a comprehensive solution that simplifies sports court reservations, optimizes the organization of games and training sessions, and provides coaches with the tools they need to manage their visibility, payments, and performance statistics.

Vision

More than just an app, MatchPoint aims to become a strategic ally for those who want to practice sports easily, reliably, and efficiently, reducing barriers to access and creating new opportunities for the sports community.
